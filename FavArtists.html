My Experience with music...

Music has been and continues to be an influential role in my life. As the son of an experienced drummer, who has played in a band (The Shining Scribes) throughout college and his prime, I have been exposed to music from a very young age. I love listening to music no matter the occasion and I love to perform. As one of the singers of Music Repertoire, I've been given the opportunity to perform at many venues, big and small, from a small vineyard to Herbst Theatre in San Francisco.

Not only do I love listening to music, but I love to create music. Messing around with my guitar and writing down lyrics to express myself has been a great method to cope with difficult occasions.
